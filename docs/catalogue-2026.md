- Non-corrosive and compatible with transformer construction materials.
- Helps maintain transformer efficiency and reduce maintenance frequency.

**Applications**

- Recommended for use in:
- Power and distribution transformers
- Instrument transformers
- Oil-filled circuit breakers
- Switchgear and other oil-filled electrical equipment
- Suitable for indoor and outdoor electrical installations.

**Typical Uses**

- Electrical insulation and cooling medium in high-voltage equipment.
- Systems requiring stable performance under continuous electrical load.
- New installations as well as maintenance and replacement applications.

**Performance Standards**

- Meets or exceeds:
- IS 335 : 2018
- IEC 60296
- ASTM D3487 (where applicable)

**Operating Characteristics**

- Maintains stable dielectric and thermal properties over prolonged service life.
- Low gassing tendency under electrical stress.
- Compatible with commonly used transformer materials and sealing systems.
